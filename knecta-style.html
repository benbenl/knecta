Usage

The Knecta marks include the Knecta name and logo, and any word, phrase, image, or other designation that identifies the source or origin of any of the Knecta product. Please don't modify the marks or use them in a confusing way, including suggesting sponsorship or endorsement by Knecta, or in a way that confuses Knecta with another brands or products (including your own).

Knecta Logo

The colored version of the Knecta hedgehog logo can ONLY be used on a white background. If you are using the Knecta logo on any other background color, use either the black or white monochrome logo.



Do not place the Knecta hedgehog logo over background colors that clash. If in doubt, use monochrome.



When using the Knecta hedgehog logo with other logos and graphic elements, maintain a safety space that equals 200% the size of the square around the hedgehog.

We politely ask that you don't:

  • Rotate the hedgehog mark or alter its positioning in relation to the Knecta name
  • Change the colors on the hedgehog logo
  • Overprint or obstruct any part of the logo
  • Add special effects to the logo
